MRP Description
My MRP is titled "Empowering Youth: STEM Career Exploration Through Narrative Gameplay". It is a high school STEM outreach project with the IMS. I am working with Maxwell Mo (Ya Qi Mo) to create a 2D story-driven puzzle game. The narrative itself will follow along a character who will be journeying through STEM, and the player will be able to make choices in the game that affect the pathway the character takes.
